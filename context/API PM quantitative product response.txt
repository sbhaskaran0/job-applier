Describe a product you have managed or helped develop, ideally one with data-intensive or internal platform components. What was the core user problem, how did you identify it, and how did you work with your team to solve it? (<250 words)
In my current role, I manage an API product that present our investor clients with forecasts for critical KPIs for hundreds of companies.
Through product discovery, I identified critical issues preventing the product from being adopted by quant investors; firstly, that the structure of the API resulted in restatements to the data creating look ahead bias that invalidated their models, and secondly that they did not have the semantic context to appropriately leverage the data.
To address both issues, I collaborated with engineering to design a new schema for the API, built to directly address these needs by leveraging a new methodology for handling data restatements with point in time history, working with the investment research team to create standardized dimension tables for metric and company metadata, and creating associations to standard industry datasets with external IDs.
Ahead of these changes, I worked with sales and marketing leadership to craft a GTM strategy for the updated product, resulting in a steep increase in new business deals for the product
